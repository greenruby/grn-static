Read
----------------------------------------------------
From the blogosphere or news feeds ...

*Gosh: a pluggable command shell in Go* (go - apr 3)
https://medium.com/@vladimirvivien/gosh-a-pluggable-command-shell-in-go-cf25102c8439

*Double Ruby Rainbow Bug* (rb - apr 4)
