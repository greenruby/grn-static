#013 - may 5th 2013

Edito

This week I worked a little more on the greeby project, that should help me in the writing of this newsletter. I spent lot of time exploring sinatra, mongo, and ember.js. A lot of fun, but it's not ready yet. So I made a rake task for the generation in the while. You can check what's planned (and participate) on the taskboard on Trello.
